1.6 billion rides

A story of public transportation, big data and YOU!

In 2011, New York City's subways were ridden 1,640,434,672 times, generating many billions of data points. That data is all open and available to the public at mta.info

So what can we do with it? What kinds of insights about NYC can be gleaned from it? faberNovel decided to find out.

A Snapshot of New Years' Eve

On New Year's Eve, up to one million people, start gathering in Times Square from early in the morning to the late evening to celebrate and watch the Ball drop. Could you imagine how that extra-ordinary number of people impact Times Square - 42 St and nearby subway stations?

Student Riders

Students MetroCards are distributed by schools to eligible students from kindergarten through twelfth grade. They allows three travel perd day between 5:30 a.m. and 8:30 p.m. Supposedly used only on school days, we used NYC Department of Education's calendar to take a look during holidays usage.
